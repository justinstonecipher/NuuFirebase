Our Purpose
Provide youth of all ages a way to continue to grow their love for soccer. Have the greatest competitive leagues and tournaments in northern Utah. Keep everyone entertained with events for everyone in the family! Ensure all youth have a fun time in our recreation leagues. Help our youth learn to work as a team and gain leadership skills while they are young.
The most important part of Northern Utah United is to provide a wholesome experience for everyone, and ensure that everyone can gain experience.
